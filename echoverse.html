Echoverse
Music Player App

Time: ~10 Hours

Skills: Figma Mockups, Working with Designers, User Centric Design

Contributors: Gaby Buitrago (branding) and Min Myat Thu (Logo design and UI element creation)

View On Figma

Targeting Gen Z and Millenials, we wanted to create a music player app that would be eye catching and modern but still easy to use. We took common features from existing competitor apps and constructed them in a way we thought would be beneficial to the user. We did a small competitor analysis on Spotify, created the elements and branding in Illustrator, and created Figma mockups.
